Program "Rebuild a house together"
More than 7000 residential buildings in Ukraine were destroyed or damaged as a result of the full-scale Russian-Ukrainian war of 2022.

All settlements of Ukraine where active hostilities are taking place are being destroyed. Megacities, large regional centers, cities of district importance, other cities and villages suffer. Unfortunately, this figure is growing every day.

It is impossible to determine the amount of funds for the reconstruction and repair of damaged housing while the shelling continues. But it is already clear that their volume is huge - it's hundreds of billions of dollars. Therefore, Charitable Foundation "Rebuild Together" is involved in raising funds for the reconstruction of Ukraine's cities and is working with local authorities to begin rebuilding the housing stock immediately after the cessation of hostilities.

We are ready to quickly and efficiently restore damaged housing (internal works) so that thousands of affected Ukrainians can return home and rebuild Ukraine as soon as possible.

500 applications are already being processed within the framework of the program "Let's renovate the house together". Applications were accepted from Kharkiv, Chernihiv, Okhtyrka. First of all, the repair work will be carried out in apartments where families with many children and retirees live. $2,000,000 is needed to complete the planned work.

Program "Housing for every family"
As a result of constant shelling by Russian military settlements in Ukraine, almost 7000 houses were completely destroyed. Mostly these are high-rise buildings. Many settlements are completely destroyed. Large cities such as Kharkiv, Mykolaiv, Chernihiv and others were severely damaged.
These are hundreds of thousands of Ukrainian families who have been left homeless.
The situation is very difficult and getting worse every day ...

President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky announced preparations for large-scale construction - the construction of temporary housing for those who lost their homes.

The government is already preparing a support program for displaced persons from the war zones, and calls for involvement in the construction of temporary housing for displaced persons.

Charitable Foundation "Rebuild Together" is joining the big construction and implementing its Housing for Every Family program.

The purpose of the program is to raise funds for the implementation of own projects for the construction of temporary housing using modern technologies of fast and energy efficient construction, as well as support for local government and state projects. Let's rebuild Ukraine together!

Within the framework of the program "Housing for every family" 3 projects of fast construction of comfortable and energy-saving housing in the liberated cities of Kharkiv, Chernihiv and Okhtyrka are being implemented. The implementation of these projects will provide housing for 120 Ukrainian families with children. These projects require $4,312,800.

Program "Window in every home"
Civilians are suffering in all the cities and villages where the shelling took place. Destroyed infrastructure, destroyed houses, burned cars are the consequences of Russian aggression.

As a result of shelling from rocket-propelled grenade launchers and aerial bombardment of residential neighborhoods, houses of varying degrees are being destroyed. The windows of houses are the most damaged. Houses without windows are a common "picture" of areas where explosions are heard. Almost all the houses in the largest residential area of ​​Kharkiv North Saltivka were left without windows. There are such examples in every settlement that came under fire. This is hundreds of thousands of apartments without windows. These are hundreds of thousands of residents who cannot return to their homes.

It is currently impossible to calculate the final amount of funds to provide the residents of the affected settlements of Ukraine with windows, because the war continues and the shelling of peaceful residential areas continues. But we can already talk about losses of more than 600 billion dollars. This is a big problem that needs to be solved together!

The Charitable Foundation "Rebuild Together" is involved in solving this problem and is implementing the Window in Every Home program.

The purpose of the program is to provide windows to the affected Ukrainians as soon as possible, to involve as many window installation specialists as possible in the implementation of this program. Priority will be given to families with children, socially vulnerable groups, retirees. TOGETHER we can!

More than 1,000 applications were received under the Window to Every Home program. The problem must be solved immediately. Applications come daily from all settlements where hostilities took place. It takes $1,050,000 to make the required number of windows.

Safe Territory of Ukraine Program.
Ukraine is one of the most mined countries in the world. Complete demining of the country will take more than 10 years, experts predict. Eight years of armed conflict and two months of full-scale war have turned Ukraine into one of the most mined countries in the world. Russian troops are leaving behind tens of thousands of deadly "reminders" of this. According to the government, Ukrainian sappers neutralize 4-5 thousand explosive devices every day. Retreating to the north and east of the country, Russian troops heavily mined the territory and homes of Ukrainians. According to the Association of Sappers, as of May 1, 2022, about 82,000 square kilometers of Ukraine were contaminated with explosives. This is about 15% of the total area of ​​the state. The estimated cost of clearing the area is $250 billion. Rebuild Together Charitable Foundation joins in solving the problem and raises funds for the purchase of 10 IVECO DAILY 4x4 55S18H3.0 D cars (awning with frame) for the needs of the part of humanitarian demining of the Interregional Center for Humanitarian Demining and Rapid Response of the SES of Ukraine. We need $1,015,324 to buy the right number of vehicles.
